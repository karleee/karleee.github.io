OVERVIEW

Gateau is a mobile app for royal couples who are in the midst of planning their special day and looking for a seamless way to select the perfect wedding cake. This was created as a personal project, with the objective being to strengthen my skills as a designer and to have some fun!

BACKGROUND

As part of a creative exercise, I wanted to create something unique that would exist in a fictional (or perhaps real) world where ghosts roamed about. I was the sole designer and I was responsible for the product design and UX/UI experience for the app. Whilst the project was short, some key achievements are listed below:

  • Applied design sprints to inform my process. I was able to effectively apply the design sprints process to identify the problem, ideate on the solution, prototype and implement the final product.
  • Executed end to end. Within a few weeks, I was able to create an extremely polished prototype of the main functions on the app, which demonstrates my ability to execute and ship quickly.
  • Designed with restraint. As a former front-end software engineer, I was able to identify what would be technically feasible in my design and I also drew a parallel with advanced Figma techniques like variables and conditionals to improve my workflow.

UNDERSTANDING THE PROBLEM

I conducted a brainstorming workshop with the users of similar applications in the real-world and it was revealed that:

  • Individuals preferred to filter their search results by simpler, more generic terms than extremely granular ones
  • Individuals preferred to have the option of completing the checkout process as a guest user rather than having to log in or register for an account
  • Individuals preferred to have less text heavy pages and more visual aids (i.e. photos, icons, etc.)

PRODUCT VISION & STATEMENT

From these findings, I decided as a product to identify key business goals:

  • I want users to quickly and easily filter through their search results
  • I want users to have the freedom to use my platform, whether or not they have an existing account
  • I want users to be informed while having an enjoyable experience when scrolling through my application

As a starting point, I did some market research on competitors to investigate current offerings in the market and take inspiration from particular features that I liked about each app.

I then conducted a couple of 15 minute sketching sessions to draw out my vision of the solution.

DEFINING THE MVP

Based on the sketching sessions, it was revealed that there were commonalties in the product vision. I identified the following key user stories:

  • Short & Sweet. Users can quickly filter through their search results with options that strike a balance between being broad enough to cover the overall search term and granular enough to help narrow down what the user is looking for exactly
  • Picture Book. Users can easily find the most pertinent information about a listing through clear text and imagery
  • Happy Trails. Users can roam the supernatural universe with listings available in a variety of locations and categories

In order to begin on the Figma prototype, I mocked up the main screens for the MVP based off the sketches that I created and came up with the following:

  • Home screen
  • Filtering menu
  • Search results screen
  • Home rental screen

DESIGN

I created some mockups of the basic user journey before going over it with the desired styling. I opted for a simple color scheme, based on the traditional Halloween colors, and a straightforward UI. I then created a clickable prototype in Figma.

Home screen

This screen was created to give users a good entry point into the application, where they could easily access all the main features (i.e. filtering, searching by keyword or by cateogry, & view a listing)

Filtering menu

This menu was added to give users the ability to select and apply filtering options of their choice for a desired result

Search results screen

This search index page was included to display all relevant listings to the user per their previously seleted filters or keywords

Home rental screen

This detailed listing depicts more information about available rental homes to users so that they can make a more informed decision

DEVELOPMENT

I used InkScape to create detailed slides with the project brief, mood boards, diagrams, and wireframes. For the polished designs and the working interactive prototype, I used Figma to bring everything to life.

RESULTS & TAKEAWAYS

I was able to successfully implement 75% of the designs in Figma, as well as a working prototype that gives users a more detailed glimpse into what the application would feel like. In the future, I hope to implement the remaining 25% of the screens (i.e. account page, favorites page, etc.) and to make the filtering fully functional in both the Figma designs and prototype.
